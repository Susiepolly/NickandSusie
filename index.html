Nick and Susie

Lindy Hop in London


Nick and Susie are swing dancers based in London. They specialise in teaching and performing dances from the 1920's, 30's and 40's including lindy hop, charleston and solo vernacular jazz.

You can find them teaching at Swing Partol London:

  • Brixton Bounce - 2nd and 4th Sunday of the month at the Dogstar in Brixton.
  • Swing Patrol Angel Solo - every Wednesday at the Claremont Project, Islington.
  • Swing Patrol's Skyliners - attendance by audition only.
